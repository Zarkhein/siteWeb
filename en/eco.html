Environmentally

Chez Delquignies Logistique nous avons fait le choix de pratiquer la responsabilité sociétale des entreprises pour participer à la contribution des entreprises aux enjeux du développement durable. nous cherchons à avoir un impact positif sur la société tout en étant économiquement viable

Windturbine

We have chosen to use a wind turbine to combat global warming. Wind energy is a renewable energy that does not require fossil fuels and does not generate greenhouse gases and when it is in production it does not generate toxic waste. It also allows us to power our buildings

Photovoltaic panels

We have installed photovoltaic panels, a product that can be used on the roofs of our buildings to produce 100% renewable green energy. They allow us to power our cold rooms.

Eco grazing

The grazing of herbivorous animals (goats, sheep, etc.) is an alternative solution to the mechanical maintenance of areas. It reduces fuel consumption and associated pollution.

BioGas

We have chosen BioGas, produced, among other things, thanks to the process of agricultural "methanisation". It is an energy that is entirely renewable. Our station will then be able to use BioGas to supply our new trucks and eventually all of our trucks that were running on fossil fuels.
