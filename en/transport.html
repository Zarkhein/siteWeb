Transport

Direct Lots

"Transports national et international de Lots directs"

Delquignies Transport owns a fleet of trucks in order to satisfy your needs in terms of direct lots that are equipped of ELIOT system (GPS system that allows the geographic location of the vehicule in Europe, the data exchange and also the respect of rules of control of the hours). We are present in the whole Europe and France and propose you the large frame of the services.

  • Road transportation of goods conditioned in complete or partial lots.
  • Transportation of the containers from and to the destination of North European ports.
  • Provision of the vehicles with or without drivers.

Chartering

"Des chargements sur mesure en France et en Europe, aux meilleures conditions négociées "

The principal mission of Delquignies chartering is to find the best solution to transport your goods with the respect of your restraints : cost, lead time and the respect of prevailing rules of transportation.

  • Global vision of the market
  • The knowledge of the prevailing rules
  • Adaptability to the geography of the transport
  • Transport adjusted to the conditions of your products

Overseas

"Des solutions fiables et compétitives"

Delquignies Overseas puts at your disposal the totality of its conveyance means in the whole world (Import - Export) for the sending of all the sizes by maritime and aerial transport.

  • Analysis of your needs in order to determine the possible means that will quarantee the best price/lead time ratio of transport/service.
  • Offer of turnkey solutions thanks to our different customs and transport services.
  • Adaptation of road transport to different flows, by favoring the railway and fluvial ones.
  • Possibility to transport the partial lot to the complete container.
