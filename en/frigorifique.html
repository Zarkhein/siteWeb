Activité Frigorifique

"Transport et stockage jusqu'a -18°c"

At Delquignies we have vehicles and warehouses that can accommodate deliveries at temperatures down to -18°C.

Véhicules sous température

We have vehicles capable of transporting your products in the best situations and an adequate temperature for your products.

  • Trucks equipped with positive/negative temperature group with T° recorder
  • equipped with electrical connection for CO2 standard
  • transport to France, Benelux
  • docking at our sites

Stockage Frigorifique

Delquignies Logistique has two warehouses to be able to store your products in low temperatures. the two sites are that of Rouvignies and that of Cauyez Sains du Nord

  • Capacity of 9000 pallets in SDN and 5500 in Rouvignies
  • mobile racks
  • rapid thawing chambers
  • Rooms from -18°c to +10°C
  • Control of inlet/outlet temperatures and monitoring of storage temperatures
  • Sanitary approval
  • Video surveillance
